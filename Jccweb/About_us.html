Our History

  • Jockey’s Conference Center is an International conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. Jockey’s Conference Center has held many national and international events before, such as the famous 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference & Asian-African Conference.

  • Jockey’s Conference Center first opened in 1974 in Jakarta, it first served as a national conference center, for government and public use, it was then at 1989 JCC undergo a renovation with the intention to be repurposed as an international conference center. Then at 2005 it then undergo a series of renovation again to become an international conference & convention center

  • As of now Jockey’s Conference Center have over 10 rooms & 4 different types of rooms that can be choosed, and customized as desired, for your every need.
